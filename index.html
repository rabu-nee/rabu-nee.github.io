Terms of Service

You are bound to these Terms of Service as soon as you paid for the commission(s). Failing to adhere will result in a blacklist and possible legal actions.

Disclaimer

  • The Terms of Service (TOS) may change at any given time without warning.
  • Clients (henceforth called "you") are bound to the TOS at the time of purchase however.
  • The artist (henceforth called "I") reserves the right to decline your request at any point of the discussion. No specific reason needs to be given.
  • For ease of licensing, my commissions are for PRIVATE people for NON-COMMERCIAL purposes.
  • I will NOT do commissions for corporate bodies or individuals whose identities are tied to one.

Pricing

  • If you want or need to stay within a lower budget, I can adjust. Do be aware, however, that that means I cannot/will not put in as much effort as intended. The final product may deviate from the samples shown then.
  • I reserve the right to adjust the prices stated otherwise during the discussion. This can go in my favor or in yours.
  • If I deem a design too complicated, an extra fee may apply.
  • If you cannot provide more references, I reserve the freedom to interpret strange unknown parts of the design as I wish. I may also simplify the design if you cannot pay the extra.

Client Rights

  • You may use the commissioned art for personal NON-COMMERCIAL purposes, meaning you must not profit with the commissioned art (e.g. making merchandise and selling them). This includes NFTs as well as the training of an AI model.
  • Using the art for your own promotion or in streams is fine, but you must give credit or use a watermarked version.
  • You may ask for constant updates. During the sketch process, you may ask for five (5) corrections, afterwards you may only request color corrections or small additional details.
  • You will receive the original full-size file without watermark. Upon request, I will add a version with watermark.
  • You are not allowed to claim the commissioned work as your own!

Refunds, Deadlines and Delivery

  • The commission will only be finalized once the money has been transferred.
  • You may ask for (partial) refunds as long as I have not completely finished the commission by the time of your refund request. The amount of refund shall be discussed by both parties but is ultimately decided by the artist.
  • Although I am known for my ridiculous speed, please allow me up to 4 weeksâ€™ time before requesting a refund.
  • By the time I have sent the finished work, no refunds will be given.
  • Finished is the version after all the limited requested changes; there is no need for the client's verbal agreement. Minimal changes may still be made on a friendly basis outside of the commission framework but are not guaranteed.
  • You may set a deadline for me. It can be as short as 7 days after the payment but depending on the scope of the commission, my current workload, my own personal life circumstances and/or a lack of communication between artist and client, a tight deadline may not be able to be upheld.
  • If you do decide to ghost me, the commission will be on hold until you reply and I can continue working on it. After 2 weeks of no answer, you waive your right for refund.
  • Please know, you are not required to tell me about your personal life circumstances. A simple "Give me some time" to inform me is enough. Just please do not ignore the one whose services you paid for.
  • If beforehand you give me full artistic freedom, you waive your right for refund.
  • Waiving your right for refund does NOT mean I will not deliver. I will deliver a finalized product.

Communication

  • Communication will take place wherever you wish.
  • I can only promise reliable and constant communication via Discord. Needless to say, even if my status says online, it does not mean I can always reply with no delay!
  • You are free to remove me from your friendlist after the commission is done.
  • Your first tone will determine my tone. Be formal and I will be formal; be casual and I will be casual. This does not mean I will not be professional, however!
  • When a deadline has been decided, I expect you to reply as soon as possible when I ask for feedback. If I have not received artistic freedom beforehand, I will not be able to continue work otherwise. For every day that I received no reply, the deadline will be pushed back accordingly.
  • I will still try to uphold the original deadline but you are not allowed to complain if I cannot do so.

Non-Disclosure Agreement

  • All work I do automatically falls under a non-disclosure agreement (NDA) without additional fees.
  • I am not going to share progress online or offline. I am not going to record or stream the work.
  • I am allowed to show the work, progress and/or behind-the-scenes after the work has been officially published or shared by the client themselves.
  • If the client wishes otherwise, the NDA can be voided.
  • This, however, does not mean I will share the work if I do not want to.
